A mineral-rich, unrefined sea salt harvested from the pristine waters of Baja California, Mexico. Unlike standard table salt (which is stripped of minerals and often contains anti-caking agents), Baja Gold retains its full spectrum of naturally occurring trace minerals.

This salt has a lower sodium content than conventional table salt while providing a richer, more complex flavor. The natural mineral content means you're getting electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium) in the ratios nature intended.

## Why we recommend it

- **Unrefined** — no bleaching, no anti-caking agents, no processing that strips minerals
- **Lower sodium** — naturally lower sodium than refined table salt
- **Mineral-rich** — retains trace minerals lost in conventional salt processing
- **Clean sourcing** — harvested from clean Baja California waters
- **Bulk value** — 5 lb bag with 1,500+ servings

## What to know

Most table salt is heavily processed — heated to 1,200°F, stripped of minerals, then mixed with anti-caking agents like sodium aluminosilicate. Unrefined sea salt preserves the naturally occurring minerals your body needs. See our [Electrolytes page](/electrolytes/beyond-water) for why mineral balance matters more than most people realize.
