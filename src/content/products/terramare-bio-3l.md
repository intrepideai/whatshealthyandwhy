A certified organic, unfiltered extra virgin olive oil from the Cilento region of southern Italy. Produced by Oleificio Serra Marina, a family operation in Laureana Cilento (Salerno province), this oil is cold-pressed from olives grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.

Terra Mare Bio is naturally decanted rather than filtered, preserving the full spectrum of polyphenols and antioxidants. It has a fluid consistency ideal for eating raw — salads, drizzling over vegetables, finishing dishes, or simply enjoyed with bread.

## Why we recommend it

- **Organic certified** — no synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers
- **DOP protected** — Cilentano DOP guarantees origin, olive variety, and production methods
- **Unfiltered** — retains more polyphenols and micronutrients than filtered oils
- **Single origin** — traceable to one estate in Cilento, not a blend from multiple countries
- **Traditional production** — cold-pressed, naturally decanted

## What to know

Extra virgin olive oil is one of the most well-studied healthy fats. Rich in oleocanthal (a natural anti-inflammatory) and oleic acid, it's a cornerstone of the Mediterranean diet.
